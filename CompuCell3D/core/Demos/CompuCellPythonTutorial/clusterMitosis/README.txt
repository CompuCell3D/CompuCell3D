This demonstrates the division of compartmentalized cells. 

Expected result: A single cell with a "center" compartment" and four "side" compartments grows and divides into copies of itself. 

A PIFF file is used to generate the initial cell. Several mitosis modes are provided. 

Relevant documentation: https://compucell3dreferencemanual.readthedocs.io/en/latest/dividing_clusters.html
